Full description:
This application adds Orléans Express buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from @Orleans_Express on Twitter.

Orléans Express serves Montreal, Québec, Rimousky, Gaspé, Grande-Rivière and Victoriaville in Quebec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Keolis Canada.

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-quebec-orleans-express-bus-android

This app is not related with Orléans Express and Keolis Canada.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news (Twitter)
